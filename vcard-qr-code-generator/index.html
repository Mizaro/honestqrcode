Create a scannable digital business card

  1. Choose Contact card (vCard).
  2. Enter the name and any phone, email, or company details you want to share.
  3. Scan the preview on both Android and iPhone when possible.
  4. Download the tested code and add it to your business card, badge, poster, or email signature.
Privacy reminder: the generator does not upload contact details, but the resulting QR code contains them in readable form. Share only information you are comfortable making public.

Why use a vCard instead of a website link?

A vCard QR code can open the phone's contact-import screen directly. The visitor can review and save the details without copying each field. A website QR may be better if your information changes frequently, because a static vCard cannot be edited after printing.

Does a vCard QR code expire?

No. The contact data is encoded in the image itself. The code has no redirect or subscription dependency. If your details change, generate and print a new code.

Open-source generation

Honest QR Code uses a local QR encoder and publishes its source under Apache-2.0. There is no account, watermark, or scan tracking. You can self-host the same generator for internal or offline use.
