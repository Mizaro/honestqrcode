Why use a client-side QR generator?

A QR code can contain sensitive content such as a WiFi password, private document link, or prefilled message. This tool generates the QR code locally in your browser so the app does not need to receive your input.

What can I create?

You can create QR codes for public web links, downloadable PDFs, video links, image links, WhatsApp conversations, and WiFi network login details.
